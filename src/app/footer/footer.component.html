Slide your Sites

Easily create slideshow presentations for all your online content.

Slide your Site turns every screen in your organization into a showroom with awesome projects, successful solutions and webbased presentations. You can even add traffic-, weather- and market related news to the slides.

Slide your Sites is a free web-based presentation tool brought to you by Dutch Grit. We facilitate flexibility and growth with webdevelopment, concepting and design. Checkout our approach and customer cases at www.dutchgrit.nl.

  1. Start creating a slide show by adding webpages. Choose “ADD SLIDE” to start.
  2. Choose your URL, set time it needs to be shown and select a transition, use the button below to add the slide to your presentation.
  3. Add as many slides as you wish.
  4. When you are ready hit “START SLIDESHOW” to open a full screen presentation.
  5. Under "SETTINGS" you can
    1. Download your slides to use them later or on a different device.
    2. Import any set of slides you've earlier downloaded.
    3. Delete all slides at once.
